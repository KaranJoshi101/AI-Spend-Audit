# TokenGuard Landing Copy

This document tracks the copy direction for the current landing page.

---

## Hero Copy

**Headline**: Audit your AI spend before it becomes wasted runway.

**Subheadline**: TokenGuard helps founders and operators find overpriced AI tools, unused seats, and better pricing paths with a deterministic financial audit.

**Primary CTA**: Run Your Audit

**Secondary CTA**: See an Example Report

## Problem Section

- Teams adopt multiple AI tools quickly.
- Spend becomes fragmented across subscriptions and credits.
- Enterprise plans are often purchased before they are needed.
- Finance and leadership do not get a clear view of the total cost.

## Value Proposition

- Enter your tools, seats, and monthly spend.
- Get clear annual savings estimates.
- Share a report link with your team.
- Book a consultation if the savings are meaningful.

## Trust Copy

- Recommendations are deterministic, not opaque AI guesses.
- Pricing data is sourced and maintained separately.
- Reports include exact current spend and savings numbers.

## CTA Copy Variants

- Run a Free Audit
- See Your Savings
- Check for Overpriced AI Tools
- Audit My Stack
