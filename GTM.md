# TokenGuard Go-To-Market

TokenGuard is positioned as a founder-facing cost audit for AI tool spend.

---

## Ideal Customer

- Startup founders
- Engineering leaders
- Finance-conscious operators at small and mid-size teams
- Teams with multiple AI subscriptions or enterprise licenses

## Core Message

TokenGuard helps teams find AI spend waste quickly with deterministic calculations, clear savings numbers, and shareable reports.

## Acquisition Channels

1. Direct founder outreach
2. Product Hunt launch
3. Hacker News Show HN
4. Indie Hackers and startup Slack groups
5. LinkedIn posts from founders and builders
6. Short-form demo videos
7. Referral sharing through public report links
8. YC Bookface or founder communities already used by the target customer

## Conversion Hooks

- Free audit with immediate savings summary
- Public report share links for internal circulation
- High-savings consultation CTA
- Clear annual savings framing

## Launch Sequence

1. Publish the landing page and shareable demo flow.
2. Run a small founder interview cycle.
3. Share on Product Hunt and relevant startup communities.
4. Convert high-savings audits into consultation leads.
5. Iterate on pricing, wording, and report readability based on usage.

## First 30 Days

1. Reach out to 20 founders who actively use multiple AI tools.
2. Run 10 manual audits and record the exact savings language they repeat.
3. Post one Show HN launch and one Product Hunt launch.
4. Share a before/after report example in founder communities.
5. Track how many reports are shared internally versus turned into leads.

## Proof Signals To Highlight

- Deterministic recommendation logic
- Current spend versus savings delta
- Public report sharing
- Premium results UI
- Clear reasoning for each recommendation
