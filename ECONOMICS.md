# TokenGuard Economics

TokenGuard is designed to convert audit insights into measurable savings and qualified leads.

---

## Value Proposition

- Identify tool overspend
- Reduce monthly AI spend
- Surface immediate plan downgrade opportunities
- Convert high-savings reports into consultations

## Product Economics Model

The product creates value by turning a one-time audit into either:

1. immediate savings for the customer
2. a high-intent lead for follow-up consulting

## Cost Structure Assumptions

- Frontend hosting and build costs are low
- Optional integrations use existing third-party APIs
- Core audit logic is deterministic and inexpensive to run
- Public report persistence can use Supabase or local fallback

## Revenue Paths

1. Consultation lead generation
2. Later paid audit plans
3. Team or company rollouts
4. Potential premium reporting or export add-ons

## Economic Guardrails

- Do not overstate savings
- Separate savings from spend clearly
- Keep the audit defensible and repeatable
- Use the consultation CTA only when savings are meaningful
